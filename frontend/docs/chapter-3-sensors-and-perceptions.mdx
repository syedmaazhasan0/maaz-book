# Sensors and Perception Systems

Sensors allow humanoid robots to gather real-time information about themselves and the world.

## Learning Objectives
- Identify key sensors used in robotics
- Understand sensor fusion
- Explain perception pipelines

## Visual Sensors
- RGB cameras
- Depth cameras
- Stereo vision

## Motion & Balance Sensors
- Gyroscopes
- Accelerometers
- IMUs

## Tactile & Force Sensors
Used for gripping, pushing, and interacting safely.

## Summary
Perception is the foundation of intelligent physical behavior.
