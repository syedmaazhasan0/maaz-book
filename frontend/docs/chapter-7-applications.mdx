# Applications of Humanoid Robotics

Humanoid robots are becoming more common across industries.

## Learning Objectives
- Explore real-world use cases
- Understand industrial and home applications
- Identify emerging opportunities

## Key Areas
- Healthcare assistance  
- Education & research  
- Industrial labor support  
- Customer service  
- Search & rescue operations  

## Summary
Humanoid robots are transforming industries by improving efficiency and safety.
