# Actuators, Motors & Mechanical Systems

Robotic actuators convert AI instructions into physical movement.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand actuator types
- Identify different motor systems
- Explain how movement is controlled

## Types of Actuators
- Servo motors  
- Hydraulics  
- Pneumatics  

## Human-Like Mechanical Structure
Humanoids often use jointed limbs similar to:
- Shoulders
- Elbows
- Knees

## Summary
Mechanical systems bring AI-driven behaviors into the physical world.
