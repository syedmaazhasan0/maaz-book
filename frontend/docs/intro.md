# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Overview

This book provides a complete and structured introduction to **Physical AI** and **Humanoid Robotics**, exploring how artificial intelligence is integrated into physical systems capable of sensing, moving, understanding, and interacting with the real world.

You will learn how humanoid robots combine:

- Physical AI for real-world reasoning  
- Advanced sensors for perception  
- Motors and actuators for human-like movement  
- AI models for decision-making and control  
- Human–robot interaction systems  
- Real-world applications and future innovations  

This book is designed for students, enthusiasts, and beginners entering the world of robotics and embodied AI.

## Getting Started

This book will guide you through:

1. Understanding Physical AI and humanoid systems  
2. Learning how sensors, actuators, and mechanics work  
3. Exploring AI algorithms used in modern robots  
4. Studying locomotion, balance, and movement control  
5. Understanding communication and interaction with humans  
6. Discovering practical applications of humanoid robots  
7. Learning future trends and challenges in robotics  

Each chapter is written with clear explanations, diagrams, and structured learning objectives.

### What you'll need

- Basic understanding of computers  
- Interest in AI, robotics, or engineering  
- Curiosity about how intelligent machines work  

No advanced math or coding experience is required—this book is friendly for all levels.
