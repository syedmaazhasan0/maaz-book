## What is Physical AI?
Physical AI refers to artificial intelligence embedded in machines that can sense, move, and interact physically. Unlike digital AI systems, these models operate in real environments.

## What Are Humanoid Robots?
Humanoid robots mimic human structure and behavior, enabling natural interaction in human-designed spaces.
