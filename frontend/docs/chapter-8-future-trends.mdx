# Future Trends in Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

This chapter explores upcoming breakthroughs and long-term possibilities.

## Learning Objectives
- Recognize emerging technologies  
- Understand the evolution of Physical AI  
- Identify long-term challenges  

## Emerging Innovations
- Multimodal AI integration  
- More energy-efficient actuators  
- Long-duration autonomy  
- Emotion-aware AI systems  

## Challenges
- Cost  
- Battery limitations  
- Safety & reliability  

## Summary
Physical AI and humanoid robotics are advancing rapidly and will play a major role in future societies.
